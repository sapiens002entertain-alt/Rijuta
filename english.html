Silence at New Year They Don't See Where is Humanity?
Silence at New Year

This is the time when I realise

That I've lost another year

Got some experiences

Some knowledge

It was a learning

Like a chapter of my life

Everyone will enjoy

Sky'll be full of crackers

They won't care of pollution

Cakes'll be cut at 12am

Children, adults & elders

All would be feeling it

In their own way, but happily

And Me! Gazing at the stars

In the nightsky

Which are blurred now!

I am Human, with most complex

Thing in my head - but

Deep down, I feel empty

It's all quiet here

Which I wanted to be out there

A sense of self deliberation

About life, existence, universe

Or the consciousness

All are one, all the same!

The quality is there ~ potential

To be aware, to be really conscious

But what do I do of Biases!

There is a Silent Acceptance

If I am Aware, then They Are...!

They Don't See

I am acting always...

To be someone else

The one who skips

To not argue anymore


They don't see me

When I am in front of them

When I am talking to them

I am preserved in myself

In my existence...

Only my notebook knows

Who am I?


They judge my principles

Like measuring depth of ocean

From surface!

I let them!


Where is Humanity?

Under the Sun,

Having supervision, as Earth,

There is a single species,

But with enormous population,

Destroying everything.


I am also in one of them,

Losing humanity and

a hope to see everyone cheery.

Personalities are only for

their own interests,

Not for anyone's help.


Some are gentles here,

But Some of them are depressed

by negativity around,

And fewer fight for truth,

For those who really want,

A territory far away from

Such a Tragedy.


All showing off a support,

No one has mercy

Over creatures, even over himself.


Where has Humanity gone?

Where to have a hope?

Yet there is no answer,

But there is single one, Me

Over whom, I can have belief.
